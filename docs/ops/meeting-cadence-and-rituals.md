# Meeting Cadence and Rituals

## Weekly Rhythm

| Meeting | Time | Attendees | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sprint planning | Monday, 45 min | all four team members | sprint goal, committed issues, owners |
| Async standup | weekdays by 09:30 SAST | all four team members | yesterday, today, blockers |
| Tech and design sync | Wednesday, 30 min | CTO, Full Stack Lead, UI/UX Frontend Lead, CEO if needed | cross-functional decisions and unblockers |
| Demo and retro | Friday, 45 to 60 min | all four team members | shipped work, carryover, one process improvement |
| Roadmap review | monthly, 60 min | CEO, CTO, leads | priority changes, risks, release outlook |

## Standup Format

Post in WhatsApp using:

- `Yesterday:`
- `Today:`
- `Blockers:`
- `Need review from:`

## Meeting Rules

- agenda owner posts context before the meeting
- decisions are written into GitHub within 24 hours
- meetings end with owners and due dates
- if a meeting has no decision to make, cancel it and keep the time for delivery
